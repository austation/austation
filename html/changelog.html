13 May 2020

AustationBot updated:

  • Added ability to move and plant mines, as well as disarm them with a multitool
  • Added ability for mines to be "smart" and detect mindshield implants if a particular mine type
  • Clown and stun mines can now be printed in the service and security protolathes respectively
  • three new mine related tech nodes, and three new printable smart mine types for security
  • Added the exploding rubber duck as a 4TC traitor and clownops item, with a more powerful version available for 10TC.
  • Some mines can be ordered from cargo
  • stun mines now do stamina damage instead of a hardstun
  • the upgrade wand, a magician only traitor item that upgrades his gear
  • lowers teleglove range by 1
  • bottomless top hat now kidnaps non-carbons faster, and works better on the user
  • removed confusing and poorly named preference type.
  • reverts cult stun buff

12 May 2020

AustationBot updated:

  • Research Servers now actually generate points, and are sensitive to heat.
  • shields can be repaired with a welding tool
  • normal briefcases can now block like secure ones can
  • fire axe is now worse at blocking, but better at dismemberment and breaking through shields and blocks
  • changes up dismemberment calculations to work far better.
  • powerfist is no longer snowflake bullshit that fucks the entirety of all melee combat conventions by bypassing shields and armor completely, but it now actually works as a destroyer of shields and the true best melee weapon in the game (not really)(but close)
  • penetrating projectiles now bypass shields
  • projectiles deal full damage to blocks
  • nerfed toy katana
  • nerfed chairs
  • nolimbdisable doesnt let you block forever
  • stamina healing drugs make you far worse at blocking
  • cult dagger no longer blocks as if it's a longsword, cult shield is now less durable
  • energy shield is now repaired when you turn it back on after it breaks. However, after breaking, there is a cooldown before you can use it again
  • double esword spin is now less theatrical, making it no longer disrupt your blocks.
  • active blocking now works as intended
  • projectile blocking now works as intended
  • Apids are now given beefriend trait, like flypeople, and are no longer stung by them.

JDawg1290, zxaber updated:

  • mech construction refactored
  • You can now replace the scanning modules and capacitors on a mech. Just use your ID on the mech after opening the panel during maintenance procedures to remove the part you want to replace, and then insert the new part afterwards.
  • Replacing a power cell on a mech no longer uses a screwdriver, and functions the same as the replacement steps for scanning modules and capacitors.
  • Mechs removed from the game via means other than damage no longer leave a wreckage.

10 May 2020

AustationBot updated:

  • Optimized radiation a little
  • Added apids, also known as bee people.
  • Added a whole lot of shit related to apids.
  • Mindshields instead of ear protection now protect from cult stun. If they're mindshielded instead it makes them drop items they are currently holding, applies a dizziness effect, a brief knockdown and does some stamina damage.
  • Antags can now have the ability to not delay the end of the round
  • Added Safety Moth(TM), and Safety Pill(TM?) posters.
  • Updated contraband.dmi to add new poster icons.
  • Special antagonists no longer randomly spawn mid round.
  • Added code to stop events automatically getting added to the event control
  • Conveyors work again.
  • Fixed Apidite, supposedly.
  • Fixed apid wing sprites.
  • HOP cant open gimmick jobs anymore
  • Plasmamen helmets no longer have flash/welding protection by default. Instead they have a toggleable visor which acts like a normal welding helmet. It being active can be noticed on the sprite.
  • Guardians/holoparasites are no longer seperated into types
  • Instead, you get to "build" one, with a limited amount of points, selecting different stats and abilities
  • The following major abilities are available to holoparasites: Assassin, Explosive, Frenzy, Gravitokinetic, The Hand, Healing, Predator, Scout, and Time Erasure.
  • The following minor abilities are available to holoparasites: Surveillance Snares, Teleportation Pad
  • Holoparasite types that didn't make the cut: Toy, Lightning, Protector, Dextrous, Charger, Chaos
  • no more random confusion when hit in the head
  • dismemberment now uses a calculation reliant on weapon size, weight, sharpness, and force to determine when a limb is lost, instead of RNG.
  • no more RNG on punches! punches now have fixed damage and no stun.
  • totally changes up block chance. blocking now relies on items being held in the active hand or not, if you are walking or not, and the direction you are facing. Shields now use durability and all other items deal stamina damage to the arm holding them when blocking. Read the pr for more in-depth info.
  • Corrects tg references in the README
